OPERATORS

ARITHMETIC

Arithmetic operators are +, -, *, /, %.

RELATIONAL

Relational operators are =, ==, ===, <, >, <=, >=, !=.

LOGICAL

Logical operators are &&, ||, !.

INCREMENT/DECREMENT

Increment/Decrement operators are i++, i--.

SPECIAL OPERATOR

Special operators are -=, +=, *=, /=.

BITWISE OPERATOR

Bitwise operators are AND, OR, NOT.
